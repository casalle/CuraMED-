Simple Process

How CuraMED works

Getting medical care at home has never been simpler. Three easy steps.

01

Book Your Appointment

Fill out our quick booking form or WhatsApp us directly. Share your preferred time slot and the service you need.

02

Confirm & Get Matched

We match you with an available doctor or counselor and confirm your exact appointment time. A payment invoice is shared — your appointment is confirmed once payment is received.

03

Care at Your Door

For in-person visits, your doctor arrives at your home fully prepared. For online consultations, your confirmed schedule with the doctor is shared and your session begins at the agreed time.

Privacy & Terms

1. Information we collect

We collect the information you provide when booking — name, phone number, address, and the reason for your visit — along with any medical details shared with our doctors during a consultation.

2. How we use it

  • To dispatch a doctor and confirm your appointment.
  • To maintain a confidential medical record for your future care.
  • To send appointment reminders and follow-up messages.

3. Confidentiality

All medical information is treated as strictly confidential and handled in accordance with PMDC professional conduct guidelines.

4. Data security

Records are stored on secure servers. You may request a copy or deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@curamed.pk.
